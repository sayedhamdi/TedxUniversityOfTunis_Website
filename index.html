Ted ?

TED is an annual event that brings together the minds leading thinkers and doers to share ideas that matter in any discipline : Technology, Entertainment, Design, Science, Humanities, Business, Development,etc. The talks at the conference, called TED Talks, are then made available to watch for free on

TEDx ??

In the spirit of "ideas worth spreading," TED has created TEDx a program of local and self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks videos and live speakers combine to spark deep discussions and connections. Our TEDx event is not organized by TED Conferences , but is operated under a license from TED.
